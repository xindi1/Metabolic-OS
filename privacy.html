MetabolicOS Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

MetabolicOS is a local-first health tracking prototype. Entries you create are stored on your device using local browser storage unless you choose to export or share them.

Data Stored

The app may store your daily domain scores, notes, optional metrics, and history on your device.

Data Sharing

MetabolicOS does not sell your data and does not transmit your entries to a server in this prototype.

Health Disclaimer

MetabolicOS is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical questions.

Contact

For support, contact the app publisher through the support email listed in the app store or support page.
