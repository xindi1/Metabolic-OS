MetabolicOS Support

MetabolicOS helps you track four lifestyle domains that support metabolic health: Nutrition, Movement, Recovery, and Body Signals.

Common Help

Data storage: Your entries are stored locally on your device.

Export: Use the Export button to download your data as JSON.

Import: Use the Import button to restore a prior JSON export.
